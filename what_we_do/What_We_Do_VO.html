01

What is a Virtual Office?

Virtual office is a service offering that gives individuals and companies access to a professional business address without actually renting a physical office space. We help businesses launch and scale at a fraction of the cost. Clients get government-compliant documents along with mail & package handling services.

In India, you can't get a GST number, register a company, or even open certain bank accounts without a registered business address in the relevant state. That address requirement used to force businesses to either lease real space they didn't need yet, or use a friend's or relative's address informally (which creates compliance risk down the line). VO solves this directly — it's the address requirement, sold as a service, without the office attached to it.

A Virtual Office from myHQ can include:

  • Professional business address — at a branded workspace location, so the business has a credible address on its invoices, website, and registrations rather than a residential one.
  • Support for GST Registration — register for GST in any state using the address. This is the single most common reason a client buys a VO.
  • Support for Company Registration — use the address as your registered office for MCA/ROC filings.
  • Mail & package handling — receive and forward business correspondence, so the client doesn't need anyone physically present at the address.

Why choose myHQ? Hassle-free, 100% digital process — no branch visits at any stage. Super fast documentation, so a client isn't stuck waiting weeks for a registration that's blocking their business launch. Verified partners and full transparency, which matters because a VO address that fails verification can get a client's GST registration rejected. 300+ premium addresses across 25+ cities, covering 20 states. And a money-back guarantee, which removes the risk for a client buying a service they've likely never bought before.

VO = real address, real registration, no physical space — 100% digital, with a money-back guarantee. Perfect for businesses that need credibility before they need a desk.

Pre-Purchase Journey

Buying a Virtual Office on myHQ is a fully digital, four-step process:

1. Discovery
Find VO across the country based on criteria: state, location, price, plan.
2. Choose a Workspace
Compare and choose a space you like based on your preferences.
3. Select a Plan & Tenure
Plans and tenures according to user needs — GST, registration, address-only.
4. Checkout
Complete payment and start post-purchase KYC.

The state and city selected in Discovery isn't a preference — it's usually fixed by where the client needs to register for GST or incorporate, so this step is really about finding the right address within a constraint, not browsing for the nicest option. The plan selected in step 3 also determines which documents the client will need to provide at KYC, which is why getting the plan right upfront avoids back-and-forth later — a GST-only plan needs different paperwork than a full company registration plan.

Discovery → Choose → Select Plan → Checkout. Fully digital, no branch visits — the client can complete this end to end on the app or website.

03

Post-Purchase Journey

Once a client purchases, the relationship is managed through four ongoing processes:

1. Client Dashboard
Client dashboard to keep track of the subscription — complete digital KYC and signature.
2. Supply Dashboard
Auto-generation of documents to reduce operational bandwidth, plus real-time updates and a data overview of all subscriptions.
3. Subscription Fulfillment
All documents shared in minimum TAT on the dashboard.
4. Renewal Management
Renewal of client subscriptions before the plan expires.

The subscription status moves through stages visible to both the client and the supply partner: KYC Verification → Agreement Signature → Processing Documents → Active → Expired.

This matters operationally because VO isn't a one-time sale — it's a subscription that needs to stay valid for as long as the client's GST registration or company registration depends on it. If a client's VO lapses because a renewal was missed, their registered address effectively stops being valid, which can put their GST compliance at risk. That's why the Supply Dashboard auto-generates documents (utility bills, NOCs, agreements) on a recurring basis instead of relying on someone remembering to regenerate them manually, and why Renewal Management exists as its own dedicated step rather than being left to the client to notice.

VO doesn't end at checkout — the supply dashboard auto-generates documents and the client dashboard tracks KYC, agreement, and renewal status in real time.

Who Needs Virtual Office

All types of businesses need Virtual Office — demand cuts across industries and business sizes:

  • Startups: Launch in a new city with a credible address before committing to physical office space. A founder can register a company and get GST sorted in days, then decide on real office space only once the business actually needs it.
  • Corporates: Establish a regional entity or satellite presence in a new city as they expand — without hiring a local team or signing a long lease. A lot of corporates need this as they expand to different cities, especially when entering a new state purely to be GST-compliant for sales there, not because they need headcount on the ground.
  • E-commerce sellers & Freelancers: Many platforms require a GST number and registered address in the state where the seller operates. An e-commerce seller shipping from a warehouse in one state but selling pan-India may need GST registrations in several states — VO makes that scalable instead of requiring a real office in each one. Freelancers use VO to keep personal and professional addresses separate, which also looks more credible to clients on invoices.

Clients myHQ has served include FLiX, Hitachi, YourStory, Dolby, Bombay Shaving Company, NOTO, Lion Air, Unicorn (Apple Premium Reseller), Mahindra Business Solutions, Dream11, Swiggy, Spigen, TEQO by Mahindra, BookMyShow, CBRE, and Ford — spanning new-age D2C brands, large enterprises, and everything in between, which is the clearest signal that VO demand isn't tied to company size or industry, only to the need for a compliant address.

If a business is expanding to a new city or needs a GST address, they need Virtual Office — that covers most growing businesses, from early-stage startups to large corporates like Ford and CBRE.
