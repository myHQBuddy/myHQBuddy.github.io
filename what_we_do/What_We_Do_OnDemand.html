On-Demand is myHQ's pay-per-use workspace product — FlexiPass, meeting/training rooms, private cabins and event spaces. It lets anyone, from a freelancer needing a focus day to a company running a recruitment drive, book premium coworking space by the day or by the hour, with no long-term lease. It is myHQ's highest-volume, highest-frequency product.

Learning Outcome: Understand what On-Demand is, the four key benefits it offers, and who typically uses it.
01

What is On-Demand (OD)?

On-Demand enables users to book workspaces by the day without any long-term commitments. There's no lease, no security deposit, no notice period — just book and show up.

Key benefits:

  • Pay-per-day usage: Only pay for the days you actually use the service.
  • Flexible locations: Access a wide range of brands and cities through one network.
  • Instant booking: Book quickly and easily through the app or web.
  • Best pricing: Get the best deal since this inventory is perishable.

Target audience:

  • Individuals: Freelancers and solo workers.
  • Small Teams: Groups collaborating on a project.
  • Startups: New businesses needing flexible space.
  • Ad-hoc Work: Temporary or occasional tasks.
  • Meetings: Professional spaces for client interactions.
  • Training & Events: Workshops, seminars, and gatherings.
On-Demand = flexible workspace, booked by the day or hour, at the best price because the inventory is perishable. No commitment, no lease.
Learning Outcome: Know the five OD products, who each is built for, and the booking unit (day vs. hour) for each.
02

OD Products

The On-Demand product covers a wide range of use-cases — from solo workdays to large team events — across premium coworking spaces. There are five distinct products:

Day Passes & Executive Day Passes
Working moms, hybrid teams, professionals without an office. Book by the day.
Private Cabins
Hybrid/sales team catch-ups, brainstorming sessions, teams without an office. Book by the day.
Meeting Rooms / Board Rooms
Interviews, board meetings, client presentations. Book by the hour.
Training Rooms
Upskilling, recruitment drives, on-ground sales team training. Book by the hour.
Event Spaces
Video shoots, event launches, meetups, conferences. Book by the day.
Day-rate products (passes, cabins, events) suit longer occupancy; hour-rate products (meeting & training rooms) suit short, high-intensity use. Match the product to how long the client will actually be in the room.
Learning Outcome: Explain the difference between B2C and B2B2C access, and why both run on the same supply.
03

B2C & B2B2C Offerings

On-Demand is built for both individuals and corporate users. It serves both B2C and B2B2C users on the same supply network, but with tailored pricing, services, and different workflows.

B2C has:

  • Instant booking
  • Flexible usage
  • Pay per go payment option
  • App-focused experience

B2B2C has:

  • Wallet credits
  • Centralized billing
  • Admin controls on employee usage
  • Employee leaderboards
Same spaces, same supply network — but B2C is self-serve and pay-as-you-go, while B2B2C runs on company-purchased credits with admin controls and central billing.
Learning Outcome: Know the three OD platforms, which user type each serves, and the scope of the WeWork India partnership.
04

Platforms & the WeWork Partnership

myHQ operates On-Demand across three platforms:

  • myHQ Mobile App: Day Pass, Meeting Room, and Event Space booking — discovery, booking and payments, and bookings history in one app.
  • myHQ Website: Filter by product, city, brand, and price range. Compare offers and get the best price before booking.
  • Team Flexi Dashboard: Invite users, create groups, purchase plans, and track credits — all from one corporate dashboard.

We have exclusive rights of WeWork India. myHQ is the exclusive partner for all of WeWork India's on-demand and virtual office business. We manage the entire tech backend for WeWork India to fulfil this business — and all bookings, even the ones placed directly on the WeWork India website or app, are managed by us.

Three platforms, one supply network. And every WeWork India on-demand/VO booking — wherever it's placed — runs through myHQ's backend.
