05

Non-Negotiables

1. POSH Training — Mandatory Before First Salary

myHQ has a Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) policy in line with the Sexual Harassment of Women at the Workplace Act. Training is conducted by their partner, Serein. It is a 45-minute online session and must be completed within one month of joining. Your first month's salary will not be processed until POSH training is complete. This is non-negotiable.

2. Anti-Bribery, Gifting & Moonlighting

  • Bribery: Absolute zero tolerance. Any act of bribery — to government officials, commercial parties, or space admins — results in immediate termination. All pending bonuses and ESOPs lapse instantly.
  • Giving gifts: Allowed (chocolates, stationery, etc.) but no cash, gift vouchers, or alcohol. Any gift above ₹1,000 needs management approval. No gifts to government officials.
  • Receiving gifts: Any gift above ₹1,000 also needs approval. Receipt of alcohol bottles or gift vouchers is not allowed.
  • Moonlighting / Additional income: Any consulting, freelancing, or mentorship income must be reported to the Finance Controller before you begin. Once approved, you may proceed. Income from your own capital investments is exempt.
POSH training before your first salary — no exceptions. Zero tolerance on bribery: it means immediate termination. These are the lines you never cross.
